HOW IT WORKS | LIVE MODE


PLAY LIVE

Once the arrangement is ready, the Groovemu Reaper Engine turns it into a live playable arranger system.
The PLAYER performs active VAR and FILL regions while Groovemu Live Transposer handles live harmony and accompaniment control. 

Here you can:

* Trigger variations & fills live
* Control the musical flow
* Guide harmony with your left hand
* Play solos with your right hand
* Transpose, adjust tempo & play rate
* Adjust split point for accompaniment and solo
* Set the global song key
 
Your arrangement becomes a real-time musical performance.

!!! IMPORTANT WORKFLOW NOTES

* Do not edit MIDI inside the PLAYER region
* Build and edit everything inside VAR & FILL regions
* Do not rename Master track names

All arranger workflow happens inside child tracks of these 3 Master tracks:

1. RHYTHM tracks
2. ACCOMP tracks (Left Hand)
3. SOLO tracks (Right Hand)

In Live Mode, all arranger playback happens inside the PLAYER.

SMART TRACKS & HEALTH SYSTEM


AUTO FX

If you place your own tracks under the project Master tracks, the system automatically applies important plugins, FX and workflow elements for you.
Just build your arrangement normally and GROOVEMU handles the rest. 🙂


TRANSPOSE

When your MIDI items first interact with Groovemu Live Transposer, the system automatically selects the most suitable Transpose Mode, Timing and Chord Switch settings for you. You can always fine-tune them later to match your own workflow.
 
Check the transpose cheat sheet. 


HEALTH SYSTEM 

Starting from an empty REAPER project or accidentally broke something? 🙂

The built-in HEALTH system can automatically create, rebuild and reconnect important GROOVEMU workflow elements with a single click.

This includes:

* Master tracks & track names
* Child tracks
* Required FX and correct FX order
* Important plugins
* GROOVEMU settings
* VAR & FILL setup for empty projects

 One click and the system is ready again.

8 YEARS OF EXPERIMENTS & FAILURES | WHY? 


I’m an audio and computer music nerd from Estonia who has always loved building weird DIY music systems and experimenting with creative workflow ideas.
For years, I loved the freedom of REAPER… but missed the live musical feeling of real arranger workstations.

Arrangers were fun to play. DAWs were powerful to edit. But neither combined both worlds. 

The idea behind GROOVEMU actually started around 2018.
The early versions were primitive, unstable and incomplete.
Many experiments failed and the whole project was paused multiple times.

But over the years, better tools, new technical possibilities and endless testing slowly helped everything finally come together.
And eventually… GROOVEMU started behaving exactly the way I originally imagined it.

A real live arranger workflow inside REAPER was born.
